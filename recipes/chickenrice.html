Chicken Rice

Description

Chicken Rice, Singapore's national dish

A timeless, homely taste

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • jasmine rice
  • aromatics and herbs
  • oil
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. poach the chicken
  2. rub chicken with sesame oil
  3. cook rice in rice cooker in poaching liquid and aromatics
  4. make ginger-garlic chili sauce
  5. make sambal kicap
  6. chop up the chicken and serve with rice
