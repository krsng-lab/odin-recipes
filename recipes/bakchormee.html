Bak Chor Mee

Description

Bak Chor Mee, a local's favourite

Perfect for breakfast, lunch and dinner

Ingredients

  • thin egg noodles
  • merinated minced pork
  • braised mushroom
  • crispy lard
  • fish balls
  • soy sauce
  • fish sauce
  • chinese black vinegar
  • chili paste

Steps

  1. cook the pork, mushrooms and fishballs
  2. blanch the noodles
  3. mixed the noodles with the sauces
  4. plate with the pork, mushrooms and fishballs
